1. `internal/strategy/bootstrap/bootstrap.go:227` and `internal/strategy/bootstrap/bootstrap.go:462` leave stale bootstrap temp refs behind for branches that are now classified as `subsumed`. `planBatches` skips populating `TempRef`/`ResumeHash` for those branches, and `executeBatched` short-circuits straight to `PushCommands` on the final target ref without running any of the existing stale-temp cleanup logic at `:252-285` or the final delete at `:385-387`. A reproducible case is an interrupted bootstrap that left `refs/gitsync/bootstrap/heads/feature`, followed by a rerun where `feature` is now reachable from trunk and gets marked subsumed. The rerun succeeds, but the temp ref is never deleted. That leaves bootstrap garbage on the target indefinitely and bypasses the branch’s normal resume/cleanup path. I’d treat this as a real regression; it needs either explicit cleanup in the subsumed path or carrying `TempRef`/`ResumeHash` through subsumed batches too.
